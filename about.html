The Story

OUR NAME CAME FROM THE SANSKRIT LANGUAGE WHICH
POSSESSES THE MEANING OF “DESIRED FORM” THAT
ALSO REFLECTS OUR DESIRE TO RISE AS A RESPECTED
CREATIVE GROUP. ON THE OTHER HAND, "KAMARUPA"
WORD ALSO COMES FROM TWO COMPOUNDING
INDONESIAN WORDS, “KAMAR” AND “RUPA” THAT
CAN ALSO BE INTERPRETED AS AN ART ROOM.
